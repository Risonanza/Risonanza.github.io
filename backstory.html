Back Story

The international theme and
some of the important places in my background
that help to explain where I am from. I have travelled a lot!

Paris

My late mother was born in Paris. I lived and worked in Paris for six years for two American companies from Boston, MA: Prime Computer & Progress Software. Both my sons were born in Paris.

Correns - First Organic Village in France

I lived in Provence for ten years. I promoted the Fine Organic Wines from Domaine des Aspras and Les Vignerons de Correns in the UK.

Hamburg

I was a business partner with IAP GmbH in Hamburg for many years. I launched their flagship product and secured their first clients in France, Italy and Turkey. And... Yes, I did visit the Beatles museum :-)

Istanbul

I lived and went to school in Istanbul in the 70s when my father was working as Manager Turkey for British Airways. With my cub scouts group we were allowed to walk on the first Bosphorous bridge before it was opened to traffic in 1973! It was the first bridge between Europe and Asia.

New York

After graduating from the University of Sheffield, I was fortunate to get a traineeship at AVIS in New York.

Bristol

Bristol has been home in recent years. My daughter was born in Bristol. I ran the Bristol Italian Conversation Group for many years and also worked as a Volunteer at the Wiltshire Music Centre in nearby Bradford-on-Avon.

Zurich

I lived and attended school in Zurich when my father was British Airways manager in Zurich. In one of the schools all teaching was in Swiss-German!

Geneva

My grand-mother lived in Geneva for 50 years and my mother studied at the Beaux-Arts in Geneva before moving to England.

Sheffield

While studying at The University of Sheffield, I joined the Sailing Club and the Theatre Group and had the privilege of working on 2 productions with Eddie Izzard as Director!
