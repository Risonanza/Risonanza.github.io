The people we work with are open-minded with a long term international outlook and a very high degree of fluency in English. They love working with people with inbound requests.

Our principal business partners in Italy are:

* Marco Barozzi. Marco is the Founder and Director of Bologna based Expo Consulting and has a team dedicated to providing services for trade shows. Expo Consulting is very well established and has been providing services to companies attending trade shows for over 20 years.

* Giorgio Guidi. Giorgio is an expert at finding distribution channels in almost any sector. He has helped companies from many countries into new markets in Italy. He is extremely efficient and a pleasure to work with. Guidi Consulting

* Ruggero Rossini. Ruggero has travelled to over 70 countries in his previous roles as Export Manager for companies in the Bologna region and now, having hung-up his flying boots, provides assistance to any company seeking to set up a base in Bologna.

Bologna is an ideal place from a logistics point of view. Bologna airport is connected to most major cities in Europe and for example has twice daily flights to Istanbul as well as many per day to London. Bologna airport serves over 8 million passengers per year and is soon to be connected directly to the city centre by mono rail.  The Frecciarossa TGV connects Bologna Centre to Milan Centre in only 1 hour.
