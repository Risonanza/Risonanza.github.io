About Baltimore Travels
These narrated travel stories are written and narrated with a good sense of fun and joy and aim to widen horizons too. Originally written and narrated for my daughter Eleanor, they are now available to purchase. There are currently 2 CD's available and you will find details below including an audio sample of one of the stories so that you can get a feel for the style. Writing has started on the 3rd album!

Sunshine in your Suitcase

Baltimore is from the seaside town of the same name in Ireland. He has always travelled since he was very young. We are going to follow Baltimore across the many miles that he travels and he will open our eyes to all sorts of wonders. Baltimore loves to travel the world. Wherever he goes, he opens his suitcase and out pops the sunshine.

Great for entertainment when driving and round the kitchen table.

For Children aged 5 - 9
