Previously...

It all started

20 years ago, Chris was asked to work for La Fondiaria, one of Italy's largest insurance companies. At that time Chris was a database expert and brought in to work on their very large database. 

Subsequently, Chris worked in Turin for a company called WITS working with them on a project for NCR.

More recently Chris partnered with Paolo Borrelli to create seminar content to help enhance inter-cultural understanding in Anglo-Italian cross-border projects and exports. Chris & Paolo presented this to the International Project Managers Association - Young Crew at the Fiat Executive Lounge in Milan in January 2015. The seminar was of high interest and attended by 20 Project Managers from companies including Siemens Italy, Heineken Italy and Milan Expo 2015.

Chris has also worked on projects with Aptiva in Cesena near Bologna and organized an event for them in the UK to promote their technology.
