My Background
- a few excerpts from my story that resonate with me

Some of the many people I have worked with over the past 30 years who have enabled me to do what I do best and who I have learned from are: 

* Etienne Remillion at Prime France, Paris, France.
Etienne and I worked together on pre-sales projects. Etienne was the first to recognize and remark to me that he appreciated my energy and ethusiasm when working with clients.

* Philippe Arnaud at Progress France, Paris, France.
Philippe was my manager at Progress France and told me that part of the job was to run a 1 week training course once a month. Infact, I ran more than one 1 week training course per month over more than 5 years - taking me all over France to train companies in many sectors including Insurance, Hospitals, Government Agencies, Software Houses and the Food Sector.

* Xavier Martin at Progress France, Paris, France.
Xavier and I ran the first ever Progress France Summer Universtity at Club Med in Dieulefit. We had over 20 clients there for a week and introduced them to the first ever Windows version of Progress. It was called Progress Pioneers. 

* Rick Kocher at Specsavers Retail Systems, Southampton, England.
* Graham Wood at Specsavers Retail Systems, Southampton, England.
Rick and Graham gave me my first chance at a role in Project Management to run the Internationalisation of Store Systems for the 18 stores in Holland at Specsavers. 

* Klaus Erichsen at IAP GmbH, Hamburg, Germany.
* Klaus de Vries at IAP GmbH, Hamburg, Germany.
Having been made redundant from my Project Management position at Towquest/Arval in the recession of 2008, Klaus and Klaus enabled me to have an international activity which used both my strengths in pre-sales, initiation and organization to promote, inform and sell their software tools in France, Italy and Turkey.

* Donato Battistini at Aptiva, Cesena, Italy.
Donato is a real visionary innovator and a pleasure to work with thanks to his clarity of thought and systems and tools design. The Aptiva ERP software is used in many logistics companies all across Italy and is remarkably efficient thanks to the design concepts of the Aptiva AOP.


Through this life and work experience, I have realised that my strengths are in Activation. I use this strength in many ways in different scenarios:

* Pro-actively introducing people in my network to each other who have common interests and who I think would benefit from getting to know each other.

* Idea Initiator, often galvinizing my ideas into events & seminars, more often than not centred around learning and getting people together.

* Organizer/Host. Having worked as a Project Manager for Specsavers, The White Company, Towquest/Arval and HP EMEA, I have the capacity to organize and get things done. Putting together, running and being the Host for seminars in Paris, Lyon, Bristol & Milan is also a key use of my activation strength.

These strengths have emerged over the years from my previous career in training and project management in the tech sector. The advantage of these strengths is that they can be applied to many situations cross-sector and are not subject matter or expertise dependent and they can be used to bring different sectors of the economy together too.

More recently I have been using this activation strength for my Italian Business Partner search, recruitment and growth.
